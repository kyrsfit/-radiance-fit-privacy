# Radiance Fit — Privacy Policy                                                                                       
                                                                                                                        
  **Last updated: April 23, 2026**                              
                                                                                                                        
  ## Information We Collect                                                                                             
  Radiance Fit stores workout data, routines, and notes locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit personal
  information to our servers.                                                                                           
                                                                                                                        
  ## Advertising                              
  This app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. 
  See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
                                                                                                                        
  ## In-App Purchases                                           
  Purchases are processed through Google Play. We use RevenueCat to manage purchase verification. No payment information
   is stored by us.                                             
                                                                                                                        
  ## Data Storage                                               
  All your workout data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. We do not have access to your      
  personal fitness data.                      
                                                                                                                        
  ## Third-Party Services                                       
  - Google AdMob (advertising)                                                                                          
  - RevenueCat (purchase management)
  - Google Play Billing (payments)                                                                                      
                                                                
  ## Children
  This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
